# Frequently Asked Questions

## Is COSMOS cloud storage?

No.

COSMOS is a cultural archive.

---

## Is COSMOS social media?

No.

There are no followers, likes, or feeds.

---

## What is a Record?

A comprehensive long-form archive designed for preserving a complete story.

---

## What is a Record Fragment?

A concise archive designed for preserving meaningful memories.

---

## Can I preserve memories of my pet?

Yes.

Record Fragment is well suited for digital pet memorials.

---

## Can families preserve their history?

Yes.

Family stories are one of the primary purposes of COSMOS.

---

## Can organizations use COSMOS?

Yes.

Schools, museums, archives, researchers, and communities may all preserve cultural knowledge.

---

## Is payment recurring?

No.

Current Record types use one-time payments.

---

## What makes COSMOS different?

Its purpose is rediscovery rather than storage.
