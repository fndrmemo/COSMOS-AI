# COSMOS Philosophy

## Why COSMOS Exists

Human history is full of memories that disappear.

Most are not lost because they lack value.

They disappear because nobody preserves them.

COSMOS exists to reduce that loss.

---

## Preservation is not the goal.

Preservation alone has little meaning.

The purpose of preservation is rediscovery.

A memory should remain available so that someone—perhaps decades or centuries later—can discover it.

Rediscovery creates new understanding.

---

## Every life has value.

COSMOS does not judge whose life is important.

A famous scientist.

A parent.

A child.

A teacher.

A soldier.

A farmer.

A beloved dog.

Every life leaves traces.

Every trace can become meaningful to future generations.

---

## Stewardship

Users do not simply upload files.

They Entrust them.

Entrustment reflects responsibility.

The Record becomes part of humanity's shared cultural memory.

---

## Love Requiem

Not every memory must remain visible forever.

Sometimes the greatest respect is allowing a memory to rest.

Love Requiem represents respectful remembrance rather than permanent exposure.

---

## Long-Term Vision

COSMOS aims to become part of humanity's cultural infrastructure.

Its success is measured not by engagement, but by the preservation and rediscovery of meaningful memories across generations.
