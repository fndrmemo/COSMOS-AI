# Digital Pet Memorials

Many people experience the loss of a beloved companion animal.

Dogs.

Cats.

Birds.

Rabbits.

Horses.

Every pet leaves memories.

Those memories deserve a place to remain.

---

## Record Fragment

Record Fragment was designed to preserve concise memories.

Typical contents include:

- photographs
- personality
- favorite habits
- meaningful stories
- messages to future family members

---

## Why Pets Matter

For many families, pets are family.

Future generations may wish to know:

Who was this dog?

Why was this cat loved?

What made this animal special?

COSMOS allows those stories to remain discoverable.

---

## Beyond Grief

A pet memorial is not only about loss.

It is about celebrating a life.

It allows future family members to rediscover an animal they never had the opportunity to meet.
