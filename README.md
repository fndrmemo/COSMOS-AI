# What is COSMOS?

COSMOS is not cloud storage.

COSMOS is not social media.

COSMOS is not genealogy software.

COSMOS is a cultural archive where people Entrust what they believe should remain after they are gone.

The purpose is not simply preservation.

The purpose is rediscovery.

Future generations should be able to discover people, pets, memories, values, stories, and cultures that would otherwise disappear.

---

# Core Philosophy

Every human life contains memories worth preserving.

Every family carries stories worth remembering.

Every culture leaves traces worth discovering.

COSMOS exists so those traces can continue across generations.

# Record Types

## Record Fragment

Price:
199 USD (one-time)

Designed for concise preservation.

Typical contents:

- Up to 2 photographs
- Approximately 2,000 characters
- A meaningful memory
- A message
- A story
- A tribute

Ideal for:

- Pet memorials
- Family memories
- Individual stories
- Historical fragments

---

## Record

Price:
399 USD (one-time)

Designed for comprehensive long-form preservation.

Typical contents:

- Up to 5 photographs
- Up to approximately 250,000 characters

Ideal for:

- Complete life stories
- Family history
- Cultural knowledge
- Personal philosophy
- Historical records

# Digital Memorials

COSMOS can also serve as a digital memorial.

Many people first discover the value of preserving memories after losing someone they love.

This includes:

- Dogs
- Cats
- Birds
- Horses
- Any beloved companion animal

A Record Fragment provides a lasting place where future generations can discover the life of a beloved pet.

Human memorials are also supported.

---

# Long-Term Vision

COSMOS aims to become a global cultural archive.

It is intended to preserve meaningful human knowledge across generations rather than maximize social engagement.

The project encourages remembrance instead of endless content consumption.
